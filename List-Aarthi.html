unordered lists: CARS

  • MERCEDES-BENZ:


    • Mercedes and sometimes as Benz, is a German luxury and commercial vehicle automotive brand established in 1926.Mercedes-Benz AG produces consumer luxury vehicles and light commercial vehicles badged as Mercedes-Benz.



  • BMW:

    • Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, abbreviated as BMW is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles and motorcycles headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany.


  • AUDI:


    • Audi AG is a German automotive manufacturer of luxury vehicles and it produces vehicles in nine production facilities worldwide.



  • TESLA:

    • Tesla is an American multinational automotive and clean energy company headquartered in Austin, Texas.It began production of its first car model, the Roadster sports car, in 2008. This was followed by the Model S sedan in 2012, the Model X SUV in 2015, the Model 3 sedan in 2017, the Model Y crossover in 2020, and the Tesla Semi truck in 2022.



  • BENTLEY:


    • Bentley Motors Limited is a British designer, manufacturer and marketer of luxury cars and SUVs. Headquartered in Crewe, England, the company was founded by W. O. Bentley (1888–1971) in 1919 in Cricklewood, North London, and became widely known for winning the 24 Hours of Le Mans in 1924, 1927, 1928, 1929 and 1930.


ordered lists: TRAINS

  1. THE MAHARAJA'S EXPRESS:

    2. The Maharajas' Express is a luxury tourist train owned and operated by Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC).It was voted the World's Leading Luxury Train Seven times in a row at The World Travel Awards from 2012 through 2018.



  2. THE PALACE ON WHEELS:


    3. The Palace on Wheels is India's first luxury tourist train launched by the Indian Railways in joint collaboration with Rajasthan Tourism Development Corporation (RTDC) to promote tourism in Rajasthan.The train was launched in 1982.



  3. THE GOLDEN CHARIOT:

    4. The Golden Chariot is a luxury tourist train that connects the important tourist spots in the Indian states of Karnataka, Goa, Kerala & Tamil Nadu as well as Puducherry, depending on the selected itinerary. The train is operated by the Karnataka State Tourism Development Corporation and the Maple Group handles the hospitality services on the train.



  4. THE DECCAN ODYSSEY:


    5. The Deccan Odyssey is an Indian luxury train modeled on the Palace on Wheels luxury train and put into service to boost tourism on the Maharashtra route of the Indian Railways.The train, owned by travel company Cox & Kings since 2014, takes visitors on a variety of seven night, eight day trips across Maharashtra.



  5. THE FAIRY QUEEN EXPRESS:

    6. The Fairy Queen, also known as the East Indian Railway No. 22, is a steam locomotive which was built in 1855. It was restored by Loco Works Perambur, Chennai in 1997, and housed at the Rewari Railway Heritage Museum.The train occasionally runs between New Delhi and Alwar.
